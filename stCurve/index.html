Speed-Torque Curve

The equations that govern the torque generated in a motor are as follows \[\tau \propto I \implies \tau = k_{\tau} \cdot I\] \[V_{emf} \propto \omega \implies V_{emf} = k_{emf} \cdot \omega\] \[I = \frac{\tau}{k_{\tau}} = \frac{V_s - V_{emf}}{R} = \frac{V_s - k_{emf} \cdot \omega}{R}\]
